ABOUT ML LAB

The lab is formed by Prof. Nizar Bouguila who received his engineering degree from the University of Tunis in 2000 the M.Sc. and Ph.D degrees from Sherbrooke University in 2002 and 2006, respectively, all in computer sciences. He is currently an Associate Professor within the Concordia Institute for Information Systems Engineering (CIISE) at Concordia University, Montreal, QC, Canada. His current research interests include:

  1. Computer vision and pattern recognition
  2. Machine learning and data mining
  3. Image and signal processing
  4. Statistical Process Control
  5. 3D Graphics and Games
